Our Mission

Our mission is to help kids find a joy in reading again. Our magazine is free and open for all to read with the goal of encouraging kids to read. Through monthly themes and fun activities, the Arctic Reading Circle aims to share recommendations for kids and to make reading fun and engaging.

From Percy Jackson to Alex Rider, we will bring back the excitement in reading!

Submissions

Hey fellow readers!

  • Artworks (drawings, paintings, photography, digital art)
  • Short stories and fan fiction
  • Jokes and riddle
  • Puzzles (word search, cross-words, personality quizzes)
  • Cartoons
  • Bingo and Book Scavenger Hunt

Meet the team

Program Director

We're a group of college and high school students who have grown up as voracious readers. With COVID-19 closing down services like libraries and community centers, we wished to find a new outlet in which kids can come together and bond over books. Thus, The Arctic Reading Circle was born. Half reading club and half magazine, we hope to provide a platform in which we can encourage kids to read and also to inspire others through their art and writing.
